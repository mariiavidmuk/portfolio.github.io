Sherlock: Architecture Case

Analogy

Navigating through the vast expanse of geo-information is akin to solving a complex puzzle, and our city landscapes hold a myriad of interconnected tales. Imagine a detective meticulously piecing together evidence on a board, linking clues to unravel a mystery. This analogy inspired our project to transform the way we understand and interact with the intricacies of our environment.

In the world of geo-maps, information often exists in silos, scattered and disconnected. Our solution borrows from the detective's method, introducing a digital detective board linked to specific parcels of land.
