From Garage to Green Sanctuary: Rethinking Stray Dog Spaces

Unveiling a transformative concept, we set our sights on underutilized parking garage facades. This vision involves converting these overlooked spaces into safe havens for stray dogs. The design incorporates dog-friendly slopes and ramps, ensuring easy access for our furry friends.

Rooftop Transformation: A Haven for Dogs in the City

In the heart of the city, where concrete reigns, an innovative project unfolds on the rooftop of a bustling parking garage. Our vision is to create a haven where dogs and humans coexist harmoniously, transforming the cold concrete into a vibrant, dog-friendly green space. This endeavor acknowledges the challenges of a highly used parking area and aims to seamlessly integrate designated zones for both dogs and vehicles.
